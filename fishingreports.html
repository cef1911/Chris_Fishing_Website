Fishing Reports

Since April 2018 I have fished for striped bass and trout. I have caught 1 rainbow trout out of the Quinnipiac River in Meriden. I caught it on a wacky rigged pink trout worm on a 1/4 jighead the end of April 2018. I also missed two trout at the same river. The two trout got hooked on a Worden's Rooster Tail. I missed them because I did not play either fish correctly.

On Tuesday May 15th, 2018 I caught my first schoolie striped bass on a Green Acme Phoebe Lure out of the Quinnipiac River this time in New Haven, CT.
